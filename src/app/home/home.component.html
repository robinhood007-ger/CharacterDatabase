Welcome to Robin's Character Database

The Ianverse is a fictional universe created by me and named after the character of mine who started it all. (Ian Gallant)

It was created for the purpose of having a world that combines everything instead of having everything highly restricted to certain times or genres.

So from Science Fiction and Current Times all the way to fantasy worlds and western times mixed with old Japanese ones. Everything is at one place here.

The Ianverse also offers a great basic magic system with a lot of creativity available to it while also keeping it's own balancing in as well.

So no need to be shy! Click through the character examples, look up the guide to magic on the right and the magic types belonging to it.

Create your character today!

Magic is created by the use of mana. Something each being posseses in the Ianverse.

Mana is embedded in the DNA and can be rewritten to fit a specific magic type after using a spell of that sort for the first time.

The amount of different magic types one can learn is decided by their magic affinity, which is completely randomly assigned by birth and can reach from one up to four different ones.

There are two general big types of magic in the Ianverse:

Immortal Magic:

Magic inherited by Demon Families or Gods

They have special Mana to themselves which lead to very specific abilities

Cannot be transfered to anybody else and is unqiue for every Demon/God

Mortal Magic:

Magic available from birth to creatures of HumanRealm

Magic types can be freely combined with eachother

Mana can only be inscribed once
